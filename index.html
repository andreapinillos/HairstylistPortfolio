About Me

Miryam Bravo

Hair Stylist

Miryam Bravo is one of Sa-Sah Salon's most talented hair stylists. Miryam has 16 years of experience and specializes in services that include coloring, cutting, hair treatments, and highlights. On top of that, she is also an expert in waxing and facials.

Miryam works with men and women of all ages ranging from children to adult clients. Please feel free to contact Miryam via phone or email to book an appointment, we look forward to seeing you soon!
